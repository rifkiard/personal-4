Rifki Ardiansyah's Biography

Rifki is an Indonesian who lives in the city of Patriot, born on October 1st, 2002. He chose to major in computer and network engineering at SMKN 4 Bekasi after graduating from SMPN 24 Bekasi.

His Career

He started his career in the world of technology in 2018 when he was in 1st grade of a vocational high school, his career began when he took additional classes in web programming, and then he founded web programming extracurricular with some of his friends one year later.

He was introduced to an expert programmer through his teacher at a vocational high school and continued his career through his support by giving him several projects to complete on his own, and he often does projects with his friends just to hone his skills.

When he was in 2nd grade at his vocational high school, he was required to do industrial studies to get his graduation one year later. He and his friends chose to meet his expert programmer (owner of Megonesia) outside of the school and ask him to do it, and as a result, they were allowed to study at PT. Bumi Daya Plaza.

In 2021 he got his graduation from vocational high school and was employed at BoksMan Asia as a Full Stack Developer until now.
